Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start playing?

Open the app, choose your board and difficulty, then place 1 and continue using movement rules.

What if the game crashes?

Restart the app and ensure iOS is up to date. Best scores are saved on your device.

Can I play on multiple devices?

Yes, but progress and scores are stored per device.

Are there ads?

No. Gridlock Numbers has no ads.

Troubleshooting

  • Sound issues: Check volume and mute settings, then restart the app.
  • Game not loading: Verify free device storage and close background apps.
  • Repeated crashes: Restart your device and relaunch Gridlock Numbers.
