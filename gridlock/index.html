How it Works

Three rules to master.

Start from 1

Pick your starting position and place 1. From there, each next number follows the movement limits.

Follow movement constraints

Move two cells for left, right, up, or down. Move one cell when moving diagonally.

Complete the board

Fill all cells without boxing yourself in. Every choice reshapes your future options.

Play Better

Small habits, better runs.

Use move hints

Enable the switch under the board to reveal available moves and quickly spot safer paths.

Undo deliberately

Tap the latest number to undo. It costs points, but it can rescue a long run that would otherwise fail.

Save x2 cells

Route high-value numbers through multiplier cells when possible to maximize the final score.

Scoring

Reward precision.

GridLock rewards efficient planning and penalizes backtracking. This original number-logic design from Mathricks™ keeps every move consequential, so use bonus cells with intent.

Difficulty Multipliers

Easy: 10x per number
Medium: 15x per number
Hard: 20x per number

Undo Penalty

Removing a placed number deducts an extra 1 point. Use undo when it avoids a full restart.

x2 Bonus Cells

Landing on an x2 cell doubles the added number value. Route your highest-value turns through bonus cells.
