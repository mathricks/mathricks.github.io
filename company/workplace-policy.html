Working Policy

Our remote-first culture and core values.

We are a remote-first company. This means remote work is not a perk or an exception, it is our default way of operating.

We believe great work happens when talented people are trusted, supported, and given the freedom to work in the way that helps them perform at their best. Our team can work from wherever they are most productive, while staying connected through clear goals, thoughtful communication, and a strong culture of ownership.

We prioritize outcomes over hours, async communication over unnecessary meetings, and flexibility over rigid schedules. At the same time, we value human connection and create intentional opportunities for collaboration, learning, and team bonding when it truly adds value.

Our remote-first culture is built on trust, autonomy, transparency, and respect for life outside of work. We want our people to do meaningful work, grow professionally, and maintain a healthy balance while contributing to a company they are proud to be part of.
