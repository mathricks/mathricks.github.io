Privacy Policy

How we handle, protect, and process your personal data and information.

Data Collection

At Mathricks, we believe in privacy by design. We only collect the minimal amount of data necessary to provide our services and improve our puzzle games. This typically includes basic analytics and crash reports which are anonymized.

How We Use Information

The data we collect is solely used for improving the player experience. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. If you wish to request deletion of any data associated with your interactions with our games or site, please contact us through our support page.
