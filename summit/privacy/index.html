Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Mathricks, we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in Summit Numbers.

Data Collection

Summit Numbers is designed to be privacy-first. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) from our users.

  • No Account Required: You can play the game without creating an account.
  • Local Storage: Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Game Center: Leaderboards use Apple Game Center. If you choose to sign in, scores are shared with Apple.
  • In-App Purchases: Purchases and restores are processed by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive your payment details.
  • No Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics or tracking cookies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info [at] mathricks [dot] com or leave us a message via our support page.
