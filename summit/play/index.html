How Summ-it fits your day

Built for quick focus, then back to life.

Whether you are waiting for a train, cooling down after work, or sharing a challenge with friends, Summ-it keeps things light, clever, and easy to pick up again later.

Relax and recharge

Short rounds, long term fun.

Play in compact sessions that fit between meetings or study breaks. Each round is self contained, so you can stop at any time and come back without losing progress.

Play together

Share targets, compare ideas.

Use deep links to send exact rounds to friends. Everyone starts from the same numbers, the fun is seeing how differently people reach the same total.

Stay sharp

Gentle mental workout.

Summ-it keeps arithmetic light, still, it nudges pattern recognition and planning. Think of it as a calm, numerical fidget toy.

Deep links & behavior

What happens when I tap a Summ-it link?

If the app is installed

On iOS, compatible Summ-it links open directly in the app (Universal Links). You land on the correct round and target configuration from the link you received.

If the app is not installed

Your device opens this page in the browser. From here, you can go to the App Store, install Summ-it, and return to the link or generate new targets from inside the app.

Privacy & data

Deep links encode only the information needed to reconstruct a game round. There are no accounts or trackers on top of that, and your play stays on your device.
