How it works

Three steps to the summit.

Plan your movement

Move pieces exactly by their value horizontally or vertically with chess-like planning on an edge-wrapping board.

Merge and reshape

Merge identical 1s, 2s, and 3s into larger values to open space. Every turn starts from the center, reshaping your options.

Hit the targets

Your goal is to hit all four edge lane targets at the exact same time: Top, Right, Bottom, and Left.
