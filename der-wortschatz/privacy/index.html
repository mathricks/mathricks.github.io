Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

At Mathricks, we respect your privacy. This policy explains how Der Wortschatz handles information in the iOS app.

Data Collection

Der Wortschatz does not require an account and does not collect personally identifiable information in the app.

  • Vocabulary progress: selected level, play progress, practice coverage, known words, bookmarks, appearance settings, and similar learning state are stored locally on your device.
  • Speech playback: German pronunciation uses the device speech system. Mathricks does not receive recordings or speech audio from this feature.
  • In-app purchases: purchases and restores are processed by Apple through StoreKit. Mathricks does not receive your payment card details.
  • No account tracking: the app does not ask you to create a Mathricks account.

Support Messages

If you contact support through this website, the information you submit is used to respond to your request. The support form is handled by Formspree.

Third Parties

Apple may process information related to App Store purchases, restores, downloads, and device services under Apple's own policies.

Children's Privacy

Der Wortschatz is a vocabulary learning tool. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in the app.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app changes. The latest version will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact info [at] mathricks [dot] com or use the support page.
