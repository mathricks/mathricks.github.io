Word set overview

Structured German vocabulary practice from A1 through B2.

Der Wortschatz combines level-based vocabulary lists with focused grammar-pattern sets, then gives learners two ways to practice: quick matching rounds and a scrollable review feed.

Practice Tools

  • Play mode matches article, German word, and English meaning in short rounds.
  • Practice mode presents cards with article, word, meaning, example sentence, tags, and emoji cues.
  • German speech playback uses the device speech system with a German voice.
  • Filters cover letters, topics, word types, bookmarks, and known-word state.
  • Progress is saved locally for play rounds, practice coverage, known words, and bookmarks.
Learning Flow

Use the same word base in different mental states.

Fast recall

Matching rounds create a small amount of pressure so articles, words, and meanings have to be retrieved together.

Context review

Practice cards slow the session down with examples, tags, pronunciation, and a familiar vertical review rhythm.

Focused repetition

Bookmarks and known-word controls let learners keep difficult words visible and move mastered words out of the way.
