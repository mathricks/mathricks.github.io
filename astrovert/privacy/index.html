Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Astrovert application for mobile devices. The application is provided as a free service and is intended for use "as is".

Information Collection

The application does not obtain personal information when you download or use Astrovert. Registration is not required to use the app.

Location Data

Astrovert does not collect precise, real-time location information from your device.

Third-Party Data Sharing

Because Astrovert does not collect personal information for gameplay, no such data is shared with third parties by the application.

Security

Since personal data collection is not part of core gameplay, risk of unauthorized access to user data through the application is reduced.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page. Continued use of the application after any update is considered acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the application, contact us at info [at] mathricks [dot] com or via the support page.
