How it Works

Three actions, one smooth run.

Launch

Drag and release the baseball to jump from one celestial body to the next.

Steer

Swipe quickly to avoid debris and stay on a survivable route.

Protect

Collect shields and spend them only when they preserve a long, high-value run.
