Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start?

Launch the app and drag the baseball from the center to begin your first jump.

What if the app crashes?

Restart the app and make sure iOS is up to date. High scores are stored on your device.

Can I play on multiple devices?

Yes. You can install Astrovert on multiple devices, but progress and scores are device-specific.

Are there in-app purchases or ads?

No in-app purchases and no ads.

Troubleshooting

  • Sound issues: Verify device volume and mute settings.
  • Game not loading: Check available storage and close background apps.
  • Frequent crashes: Restart your device, then relaunch Astrovert.
