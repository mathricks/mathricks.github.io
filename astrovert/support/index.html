Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Q: How do I start playing Astrovert?
    A: Open the app and start the game by dragging the baseball you see in the middle of the screen.
  • Q: What do I do if the game crashes?
    A: Restart the game and ensure your device's OS is up to date. Your high score is saved on your device.
  • Q: Can I play on multiple devices?
    A: Yes, you can play on as many devices as you wish. However, high scores are saved per device.
  • Q: Are there in-app purchases?
    A: No, there is no in-app purchases.
  • Q: Are there ads?
    A: No, there is no ads. Enjoy the game without any distractions.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Sound not working: Check the device's volume settings and headphones.
  • Game not loading: Check your device storage.
  • Game crashes: Please restart the app.
